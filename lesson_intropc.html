The mid-20th century introduced existential risks‚Äînuclear escalation, global ideological confrontation, and mass civilian vulnerability. Scholars and practitioners responded by building a field dedicated to understanding peace as rigorously as war.

Irenology Defined: Eirene (Peace) + Logos (Study)
